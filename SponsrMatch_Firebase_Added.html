Process

From search to deal in 3 steps

No more spreadsheets. No more cold emails. No more guesswork.

01

Create Your Profile

Sponsors list their brand goals, audience, budget, and category preferences. Seekers list their event, reach, demographics, and sponsorship packages.

02

AI Finds Your Match

Our matching engine analyzes audience alignment, budget fit, category relevance, and historical performance to surface the highest-quality matches — ranked by compatibility.

03

Connect & Close

Browse your curated matches, send a proposal with one click using our standardized templates, and negotiate directly on the platform. Track ROI post-event.
